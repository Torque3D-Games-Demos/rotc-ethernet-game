Life as CAT

CATs are the physical manifestations of the Reds and Blues that can capture zones, tag enemies, shoot and be destroyed.
The Reds and Blues have the following basic properties:

  • Health: 75
  • Damage buffer: 25
  • Energy: 100
In addition, every CAT has a shield, an Etherboard, two Crystal Sceptres, the Trident and the primary weapon that was selected while in Etherform.

Health

Players start with 75 health points. At least 25% health is needed to manifest as a CAT. Health is lost if a player takes more damage than the damage buffer can absorb. To regain health, players can either change back into Etherform and rest in a zone owned by their team or inflict damage on an enemy CAT. If you damage another CAT, 50% of the inflicted damage is returned to you first as health and then put into your damage buffer, possibly overflowing it.

Energy

A CAT needs energy to power its shield and fire its weapons. The energy consumed when firing a shot from a weapon is restored if the shot hits an enemy.

Shield

Every CAT has a shield that can absorb up to 50% damage if the CAT has full energy. The damage absorbed by the shield decreases in a linear fashion with decreased energy.

Damage buffer

Players have a buffer that constantly re-/degenerates to 25 points. Damage first hits the damage buffer before affecting a player's health. The damage buffer can overflow if players inflict enough damage on enemies.

Etherboard

By holding down the left control key, CATs can trade agility for speed by using the Etherboard to ride the ether.

Tagging

Players are tagged when they take damage from an enemy. Tagged enemies appear on the HUD.

Returning to Etherform

By pressing TAB, players manifested as CAT can dissolve their physical bodies at any time.
