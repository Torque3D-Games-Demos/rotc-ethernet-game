Controls reference

Basic Controls

esc Toggle in-game menu
c Toggle commander view
t Team chat
z Global chat

Observer Controls

W A S D Movement
Mouse X & Y axis Look
Mouse Z axis (wheel) Zoom
y Show bigger minimap

Etherform Controls

W A S D Movement
Space / Control Up / Down Movement
Mouse X & Y axis Look
Mouse Z axis (wheel) Zoom
Tab Manifest as CAT
1 Select primary weapon: Micro-Grenade Launcher
2 Select primary weapon: Sniper ROFL
y Show bigger minimap

CAT Controls

W A S D Movement
Mouse X & Y axis Look
Mouse Z axis (wheel) Zoom
Left mouse button Fire equipped weapon
Right mouse button Fire disc
Middle mouse button Throw grenade
Left shift + middle mouse button Throw grenade at full force
Tab Return immediately to Etherform
Left shift Walk slowly
Left control Use Etherboard
1 Equip Trident
2 Equip primary weapon
q Toggle equipped weapon
y Show bigger minimap

Commander view Controls

W A S D Pan map
Mouse Z axis (wheel) Zoom
q Zoom out
e Zoom in
c Deactivate commander view

w Move camera forward (3rd person freelook mode only)
s Move camera backward (3rd person freelook mode only)

Middle mouse button Playback speed: 0.25x
