Life as Etherform

After joining a team, players spawn as Etherform. Etherforms can move freely around the map, but can't pass through solid objects. As Etherform, players can select their primary weapon, move quickly from zone to zone and manifest as CAT anywhere within their team's zones. In addition, players regenerate their health while they are in Etherform in a zone that's owned by their team.

Manifesting as CAT

In order to get anything done, players need to change from Etherform into CAT form. This is done by pressing TAB while being inside a zone that's owned by the player's team. Manifesting as CAT requires at least 25% health and 50% energy.
