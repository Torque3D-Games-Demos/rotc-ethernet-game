Life as an Observer

New players are put into observer mode. In this mode, players are completely invisible and are able to move absolutely freely around the map. You can increase your movement speed by pressing down the left mouse button, pressing down the right mouse button or - for maximum speed - pressing down both the left and the right mouse button.
