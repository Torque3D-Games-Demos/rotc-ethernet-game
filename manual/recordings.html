Recordings/Demos

(Note that this stuff is not yet stable and starting recording or playback of a demo might crash the game)

Recording a demo

Press F3 to start recording a demo. F4 stops recording.

Recording/Demo playback controls

Tab Toggle freelook
q Toggle 1st/3rd person
Space Toggle pause
Mouse X & Y axis Look around (freelook mode only)
a Move camera left (3rd person freelook mode only)
d Move camera right (3rd person freelook mode only)
w Move camera forward (3rd person freelook mode only)
s Move camera backward (3rd person freelook mode only)
Left mouse button Playback speed: 0.1x
Middle mouse button Playback speed: 0.25x
Right mouse button Playback speed: 0.5x
1 Playback speed: 0.1x
2 Playback speed: 0.25
3 Playback speed: 0.5x
4 Playback speed: 10x/td>
5 Playback speed: 4x
6 Playback speed: 2x
